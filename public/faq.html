Frequently Asked Questions

What types of cases are handled by advocates of LegalDoji?

Our advocates specialize in a wide range of legal issues, including but not limited to Civil cases, criminal cases, family law, employment law, and more.

Is LegalDoji a law firm?

Service cost/consultancy fee is decided by the respective advocates. You can log in to our dashboard and filter advocates as per your budget.

What are the other filter options available for selecting an advocate?

You can filter an advocate by their district, by their practice area, by their experience and by their consultancy fee.

How do I book a consultation?

Booking a consultation is easier than writing this, all you need to do is to sign in, choose your advocate, pay the consultancy fee and you are done.

What payment method is accepted by you?

We accept UPI, Debit card, Credit Card and Netbanking for payments.

Can I get an advocate from my local area?

You can search for an advocate from our filter.

How long will it take to resolve my case?

No case is the same and therefore every case has its own timeline to get resolved. With the guidance from our expert advocates you can be sure to get it resolved ASAP

Do you offer legal services in languages other than English?

We have advocates from every part of India be it East or West, North or South. So you can always get an advocate for your preferred language.

Do you offer free initial consultations?

No

What should I bring to my initial consultation?

It is helpful if you bring any relevant documents or information related to your case to your initial consultation. This may include police reports, medical records, or any other documentation that may be relevant to your case.

Do you offer representation in court?

Yes, our attorneys are experienced litigators and are prepared to represent you in court if necessary.

How can I contact your firm if I have an emergency?

If you have an emergency and need to speak with an attorney urgently, please call our main office number and follow the prompts to reach our after-hours emergency line. Someone will be available to assist you.
